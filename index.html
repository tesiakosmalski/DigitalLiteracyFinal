Digital Literacy Scholarship / Tesia Kosmalski / WRIT 5662 / Fall 2019
Digital Literacy Making Meaning Dynamic Delivery Digital Embodiment

Through the last semester, I found three key themes that have helped and will continue to help me understand, learn, and contextualize digital technologies. The first of these themes is the idea of making meaning in digital technologies and within the role of a technical communicator. The second is dynamic delivery and what it means to the way content is consumed. The third is digital embodiment and its place in helping understand the future of digital technologies.
